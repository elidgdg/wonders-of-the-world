Taj Mahal

This mausoleum complex in Agra, India is regarded as one of the world's most iconic monuments and is perhaps the finest examplle of Mughal architecture.

It was built by Emperor Shah Jahan (reigned 1628-58) to honor his wife Mumtaz Mahal, who died in 1631 giving birth to their 14th child. It took 22 years and 20,000 workers to construct the complex, which includes an immense garden with a reflecting pool.
